Defining Frontend Development.

Let's standardize the labels of fronted development!

Why?

Regardless of whether you're speaking with someone inside or outside of the technical community, very little is actually communicated when you say "I'm a Frontend Developer". Because the range of professionals calling themselves frontend developers is enormous, it can extremely hard to find the right dev for the job.

TL;DR

The goal is to effectively communicate our craft. Try labeling yourself using the inverted pyramid writing style. Begin with the general and drill-down to the specific.
